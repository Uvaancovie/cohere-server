Refunds & Cancellations Policy (Summary)
- Deposits are non‑refundable once work begins.
- If client cancels mid‑project, deliverables to date are handed over; remaining work is cancelled; any hours beyond deposit are invoiced at contract rate.
- Template purchases are final (digital goods). Bug fixes provided within 14 days.
- Maintenance retainers can be cancelled with 30 days notice; unused hours do not roll over.
